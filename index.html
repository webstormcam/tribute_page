Inio Asano

Creator of Good Night Pun Pun and Dead Dead Demons

Here's a time line of Asano's life:

  • 1980 - Born in Ishioka, Japan
  • 2000 - Release of Futsū no Hi (普通の日, "An Ordinary Day")
  • 2000- Release of Sotsugyōshiki Jigoku (卒業式地獄, "Graduation Ceremony Hell")
  • 2001 - Won first prize in the 2001 GX competition for young manga artists.
  • 2010 - Yomiuri Shimbun described Asano as "one of the voices of his generation.
  • 2013 - Good Night Pun Pun is completed
